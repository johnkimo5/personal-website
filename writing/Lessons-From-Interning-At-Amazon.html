Interning At Amazon

December 15, 2025

The goal is to document some lessons I learned during my first real SWE internship @ Amazon.

Ask Questions & Notes

Ask questions before it's too late. 12 weeks goes by quickly, and I was pretty hesitant to ask too many questions at the start when I should've asked more. It's also important to journal what you did, what's blocking you, next steps, etc.

I'll add more reflections when I think of them.
